About Climate Echoes

Mission, background, and why youth voices matter.

Our Mission

Climate Echoes is a youth-centered platform amplifying creative expressions on climate justice. We invite submissions from youth aged 14–30 globally, curating a living exhibition of stories, sounds, and visions for a just future.

Why It Matters

Young people are on the frontlines of climate disruption. Art transforms complex feelings into shared action—bridging climate anxiety, honoring Indigenous wisdom, and exposing greenwashing while demanding justice.

Who It's For

  • Youth 14–30, creators and first-time storytellers alike
  • Educators and community organizers fostering creative climate dialogue
  • Partners—NGOs, schools, collectives—co-creating exhibitions and workshops

What We Do

  • Open calls for multimedia submissions
  • Interactive digital showcases and touring pop-ups
  • Creative AI labs and skill-sharing workshops
